Introduction

This course provides an introduction to the Internet of Things (IoT), focusing on its principles, technologies, and applications. Students will gain knowledge of IoT architectures, devices, and communication protocols, and how they interact to create intelligent systems. The course also emphasizes practical skills through hands-on labs using Cisco Packet Tracer for simulating IoT environments.
