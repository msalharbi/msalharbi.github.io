Introduction

This course delves into the pervasive role of multimedia in daily life, covering key components like text, graphics, voice, video, and animation. It explores principles, standards, and current technologies, providing hands-on experience. Topics include development tools, techniques, compression standards, and guidelines for multimedia planning, design, production, and implementation. The course also addresses scanning processes, development, editing, and professional considerations in multimedia systems.
